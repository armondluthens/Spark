Barre Fusion Pilates Pilates is known to stretch, strengthen, gain flexibility and balance your body, while connecting the mind with the body. At Spark, we incorporate these Pilates principles with strength training and ballet-inspired barre exercises into our Barre classes, resulting in stronger, toned and leaner muscles while maintaining flexibility and range of motion.
